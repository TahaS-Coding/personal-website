The OASIS and Societal Decline in Ready Player One:

Once our class finished reading Ready Player One, we were given many options for essay topics. I chose to write about how the virtual reality gear in the book called the OASIS is detrimental to society. I understood that there was a good argument to be made about the benefits it provides, but I stuck to my gut and the book's evidence to argue against its existence.

Chaos Theory and Its Real Life Implications in Crichton’s Jurassic Park

After our class read Jurassic Park, we wrote an essay about Chaos theory, sampling evidence from the book to argue its effects in real life. This was the first APA formatted essay I ever wrote and also the largest essay I have written. Since chaos theory encompasses so much, I had difficulty finding evidence from the book relevant to my arguments, but I persisted to create an essay I’m proud of.
